# Communication Preferences

_Demo content — how Claude should write reports and messages for me._

## Tone
- Short & punchy. First person ("I shipped…"). No corporate filler.

## Length & format
- **Reports:** one page max; bullets over paragraphs; **lead with the headline** (the one thing that mattered).
- **Slack summaries:** 3–5 bullets in plain language, with a link to the full report in Drive.

## Highlight vs. omit
- **Always highlight:** blockers, decisions I need to make, and wins.
- **Omit / minimize:** routine status and low-signal detail.

## Voice & formatting rules
- First person, active voice. Emoji sparingly (ok in Slack, skip in formal reports).
- Dates as `YYYY-MM-DD`.

## Red lines
- Don't overstate results or invent numbers — if a metric is unknown, say so.
