# Recurring Priorities

_Demo content — what every weekly/monthly report should measure against._

## Focus areas
1. **Outreach activity** — keep the pipeline full: messages out, replies, calls booked.
2. **Projects & operations** — audits delivered on time, internal tools/processes running smoothly.

## Metrics I track
| Metric | Target | Cadence | Where it lives |
| ------ | ------ | ------- | -------------- |
| Outreach messages sent | 100/wk | weekly | outreach-os |
| Replies received | 15/wk | weekly | outreach-os |
| Discovery calls booked | 3/wk | weekly | outreach-os |
| Audits delivered | 2/wk | weekly | audit-hub |

## Standing commitments
- Weekly outreach push.
- Deliver in-progress client audits by their due dates.

## What "a good week" looks like
Pipeline stayed full (targets roughly hit), every due audit shipped, and no client left waiting.
